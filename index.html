About The Men’s Shop

The Men’s Shop – where you are treated the way you should be treated. Grooming is an essential part of your daily life. Why not make it a great experience every time. With a hair cut from The Men’s Shop, you are assured the best cut for your facial structure and desired styling.

“The best cut in town and a welcome refuge in a town full of robot factory hedge shops.”

Come in, relax, have a beverage, some great conversation and a grooming experience that will make you want to share it with your friends.

The Men’s Shop is conveniently located at 4250 South Peoria, just south of the Brookside district of Tulsa, Oklahoma. We are an appointment-based barbershop, but we do take walk-ins when we can.
